Pride & Prejudice

By: Jane Austen

Romantic Drama

Abby's Rating:

Finished in 3 Weeks



The more I see of the world, the more am I dissatisfied with it; and every day confirms my belief of the inconsistency of all human characters, and of the little dependence that can be placed on the appearance of either merit or sense.

A classic that challenged the patriarchy

I decided that I wanted to read all of the books that I wasn't assigned to read in high school. This was a good read, at times the language made it a little less interesting. I would suggest that people should read it since it is inspiring to see a character revolt against the expectations of a woman. Elizabeth Bennett is a badass feminist.
